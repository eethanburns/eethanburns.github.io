Privacy Policy – QuickChop for Chrome

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

QuickChop for Chrome respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any external server or third-party service.

Data Collection

The extension only stores the following data locally on your device:

-Custom sample title (for labeling exported files)

-A counter to number exported audio slices

This data is stored using Chrome’s chrome.storage.local API and never leaves your computer.

Data Usage

The stored data is used solely to organize and name your downloaded audio files. All audio recordings are user-initiated and saved directly to your device’s storage.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with any third parties or external services.

Analytics & Tracking

QuickChop does not use cookies, trackers, analytics tools, or any external logging services.

Policy Updates

If this privacy policy is updated in the future, changes will be published on this page.
