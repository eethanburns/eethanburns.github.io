The C*****e Karaoke Tape

September 11, 2025

What allowed Clipse to return 16 years later and not miss a beat? The answer became clear to me when the Guardian published Clipse's eight favorite albums.

From Billy Joel, to Biggie, to Bono. Across genres, styles, and trends. Authenticity will never be out of fashion, and good music is forever.

This is my interpretation of that thread, my thank you for everything Clipse has inspired me to make, and my love letter to the best songs that music has to offer. 8 tracks, 1 for each album.

Please enjoy, The C*****e Karaoke Tape.
